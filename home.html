Our Kingdom Mission

Matthew 28:18-20

To minister the Word of Faith, teaching believers who they are in Christ Jesus, taking them from milk to meat, and from religion to reality. We aim to plant churches within the 9 provinces of South Africa and establish a Transformational Leadership Movement in Gauteng by Vision 2030. Through partnerships (1 Samuel 30:24) we co-labor for the harvest, providing mutual prayer and financial support.

Who We Are

Acts of the Apostles was founded in 2021 in Barcelona, Etwatwa Ext 32, City of Ekurhuleni. We are a vibrant, multicultural family driven by Mark 16:15. We believe the church is a living body where every culture reflects God's glory. Our doors are open to all seeking a deeper relationship with Christ, spiritual growth, and a community that serves with love and power. From Tuesday prayer meetings to Sunday main services, we exist to glorify Jesus and make disciples of all nations.


Join us at Barcelona Ext 32, Benoni. We are a church with a vision — raising Kingdom leaders, transforming communities, and preaching the gospel to every creature.
